Who I am?

Hello! I am Alex Lucena, a Spanish web developer based in Dublin. After several years working in the education system, I decided to move forward and find my place in the web technologies area. I have been working to improve my skills to turn this passion into a profession.

A few years ago, in 2017, I started my web developer studies at Nowe Creative (Madrid), since I saw in the browser my first "Hello World", I became a code addict, that's why after finish the course in 2018, I continued learning and developing my own projects.

In 2019, looking for my next step in my web developer career, I moved to Dublin. Here, I got, with hard work, the chance to study a Higher Diploma in Sciences in Web Technologies at the National College of Ireland (NCI). After a big joyous effort and finding the balance between my work and studies, I finished the diploma with a first honoured grade.

Currently, I am developing side projects on demand and I keep learning new and excited libraries, frameworks and languages.
"It's what you learn after you know it all that counts." John Wooden.
